Make a Difference: Join the Environmental Movement

Our planet is facing unprecedented environmental challenges. Climate change, pollution, and biodiversity loss threaten the health of our planet and future generations. But there is hope. By making conscious choices and taking collective action, we can create a more sustainable future.

This website provides valuable resources, tools, and information to help you live a more eco-friendly life. Learn about renewable energy, conservation tips, reduce your waste, and connect with a community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about environmental protection.

WHO WE ARE

Creating Better Future Green Environment

Future guardians initiative is a movement that was cofounded by Michael Mboya a student at one of the Kenyan institution and aims and making a global impact on the enevironment By targetting the youths ,the younger generation, to make the environment better. We focus on planting of trees , managing of wastes ,care of the water sources and make rigorous efforts day in day out to try minimize pollution.

We as well help support people in marginal,affected by adverse environment condition, areas and also sponsor planting of trees in institutios like schools and other institutions as well focusing to make a greener future that everyone would like to live in. With cutting edge technology we are not left behind in passing education out globallly on some key issues in conservation of the environment we pass education to users through our website and our application on playstore Future guardians.
